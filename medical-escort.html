Medical Escort Services

Safe and compassionate assistance for patients during commercial flights.

Professional In-Flight Medical Assistance

viccaas provides specialized Medical Escort Services for patients who can travel on commercial flights but still need professional medical supervision. Our trained medical staff ensures comfort, safety, and continuous care throughout the journey.

Key Highlights

  • Qualified nurses or doctors accompany patients onboard
  • Medical equipment and oxygen support as required
  • Suitable for non-critical patients needing medical attention
  • Coordination with airlines and airport authorities
