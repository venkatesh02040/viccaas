About viccaas

Delivering lifesaving air medical transport with compassion, speed, and precision.

Our Story

viccaas Air Ambulance was founded with a mission to provide safe, fast, and reliable medical transport services across the globe. With a decade of experience, we have successfully completed thousands of emergency and non-emergency evacuations, helping patients reach quality healthcare facilities quickly and efficiently.

Our commitment is to ensure every patient receives the best care during their journey, with a focus on medical precision, compassion, and professionalism.

About Viccaas

Viccaas Air Ambulance Services provides rapid, ICU-equipped air medical transport with expert care, ensuring lifesaving support when every second matters across India and beyond.

Our Mission

To save lives by providing world-class air ambulance and medical evacuation services with safety, speed, and care at the core of everything we do.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in air medical transport known for excellence, innovation, and unwavering commitment to patient well-being.
